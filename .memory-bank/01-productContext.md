# Product Context

## Vision
Create a premium, tech-focused landing page that sells interview questions for mid to senior Product Management roles at America's top 30 tech companies.

## Goals
- Drive conversions (purchase or lead capture) with elegant, credible messaging.
- Establish authority through social proof and differentiated content.
- Communicate product value quickly and clearly.

## User Problems
- Candidates need high-quality, realistic PM interview questions.
- Candidates want confidence that material mirrors top tech company standards.
- Candidates want a concise, trustworthy resource without fluff.
